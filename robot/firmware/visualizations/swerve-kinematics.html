Explanations

notation:

big wheel: $\color{orange}{a}$

small wheel: $\color{orange}{a'}$

swerve body: $\color{red}{s}$

swerve wheel: $\color{red}{w}$

bevel gear ratio: $\color{purple}{b}$

circumference wheel: $C_{\color{red}{w}}$

swerve relations, forward kinematics

$\frac{\omega_{\color{orange}{a}} + \omega_{\color{blue}{b}}}{2} = \omega_{\color{red}{s}}$

$\omega_{\color{orange}{a}} - \omega_{\color{blue}{b}} = \omega_{\color{orange}{a}'} = -\omega_{\color{blue}{b}'}$

$\omega_{\color{red}{w}}={\color{purple}{b}} * -\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}={\color{purple}{b}} * \omega_{\color{blue}{b'}}$

$v_{\color{red}{w}}=\omega_{\color{red}{w}}C_{\color{red}{w}}$

deduced reverse kinematics

$\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}=\frac{\omega_{\color{orange}{a}} - \omega_{\color{blue}{b}}}{2}$

$2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}=\omega_{\color{orange}{a}} - \omega_{\color{blue}{b}}$

$\omega_{\color{orange}{a}} = 2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}+\omega_{\color{blue}{b}}$

$\omega_{\color{red}{s}}=\omega_{\color{orange}{a}}+\omega_{\color{blue}{b}} $

$\omega_{\color{red}{s}}=(2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}+\omega_{\color{blue}{b}})+\omega_{\color{blue}{b}} $

$\omega_{\color{red}{s}}=2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}+2\omega_{\color{blue}{b}}$

$\omega_{\color{red}{s}}-2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}=2\omega_{\color{blue}{b}}$

$\omega_{\color{blue}{b}}=\frac{\omega_{\color{red}{s}}-2\omega_{\color{orange}{a'}}}{2}$
